## üéì Teaching Points Documented

### For Instructors

**When Students Miss the Point:**
- Don't just grade on "is it better?"
- Grade on "did they engage with constraints?"
- Look for trade-off analysis
- Check personality preservation
- Assess meta-learning in reflection

**When Students Struggle:**
- Intervene after 2 failed attempts (not too early, not too late)
- Use "delete the branch" philosophy
- Turn crises into teaching moments
- Document the learning process

**When Grading:**
- Process > product
- Evidence of iteration matters
- Honest reflection > polished outcomes
- Perfect score = perfect process, not perfect result

### For Students (via Materials)

**What Excellence Looks Like:**
- Systematic prompt refinement (3+ versions)
- Critical evaluation of AI outputs
- Clear trade-off analysis
- Professional documentation
- Meta-learning insights

**What to Avoid:**
- Uncritical acceptance of AI outputs
- Missing process documentation
- Generic reflections without specifics
- Removing all constraints/personality
- Claiming "no trade-offs"
